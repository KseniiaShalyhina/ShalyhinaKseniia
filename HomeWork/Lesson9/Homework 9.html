We Love Traveling

Bali

From magnificent mountains to rugged coastlines to volcanic hillsides to black sandy beaches, it’s no surprise Bali is known as the Island of the Gods.

Norway

To experience the unbelievable colours that move across the Arctic sky is on many travellers’ bucket list, and few places on earth offer more ways to witness the northern lights than Norway.

Tahiti

The Islands of Tahiti are a tropical paradise so the weather is usually around 80 degrees all year round. You can swim with sharks, rays an even dolphins anytime of the year, but if you want to also swim with humpback whales you will need to visit from August through October.
